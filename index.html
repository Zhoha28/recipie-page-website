Grilled Buffalo Chicken

30 Minute Meal| Grilling Chicken Breast| 5 minutes PREP

Get ready for a taste explosion with this grilled buffalo chicken recipe! Ready to serve in less than 30 minutes , the chicken is wonderfully juicy and has all the flavor of your favorite wings.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 jar of grape jelly
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 tablespoon oil
  • 4 chicken breasts
  • 3/4 cup buffalo hot sauce divided
  • Crumbled blue cheese
  • Chopped green onion
  • Ranch or blue cheese dressing

Directions

  1. Heat the grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Combine the onion powder, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a small dish.
  3. Rub the spice mixture and olive oil over the chicken breasts. Then, generously brush the chicken with 1/2 cup of buffalo sauce, coating all sides.
  4. Place the chicken breasts on the grill and. After 6-8 minutes, flip the chicken breasts. Continue grilling for an additional 6-8 minutes.
  5. After grilling, clean the brush and add more buffalo sauce on the chicken. Serve the chicken with crumbled blue cheese, chopped green onion, and ranch or blue cheese dressing.

Recipe Tips and Notes

  • Cooking times may vary depending on the thickness of your chicken breasts.
  • To ensure that the chicken is cooked all the way through, use an instant read thermometer. When inserted, it should register at 165F.
  • Let the chicken rest for 5 minutes before serving. This allows the juices to redistribute.
